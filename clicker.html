Piggy Clicker is a Higgy Piggy Games original creation. Piggy Clicker is currently on early access and the full game will be released in summer of 2025. It is an infinetly long game just like Piggy Hunting. the aim of the game is to simply click the piggy as much as you can to make bacon. There is a shop feature and with enough bacon you can purchase upgrades for your clicker.
